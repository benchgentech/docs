# Welcome to BenchGen Docs

**The Digital Gym for AI Agents**

BenchGen helps you benchmark, evaluate, and train AI agents in interactive simulation environments. You can run agents, capture complete trajectories, analyze failures, and turn them into training data.

### Why Choose BenchGen?

- Interactive environments (browser, e-commerce, support, research, etc.)

- Full trajectory logging (thought → action → observation → reward)

- One-click benchmarks with LLM-as-judge

- Ready-made templates and custom environment builder

- SDK, CLI, and API support
