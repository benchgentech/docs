# Welcome to Agentspace

**Agentspace** is your dedicated hub inside BenchGen for managing, building, exploring, and benchmarking AI agents.

It is the central workspace where you can:

- Discover ready-made agent templates

- Build and customize your own agents

- Run benchmarks in interactive environments

- Analyze trajectories and performance

- Connect agents to each other (A2A)

### Why Agentspace?

- Centralized place for all your agent experiments

- Seamless integration with the Digital Gym environments

- Tools to turn evaluation failures into training improvements

- Full support for both no-code and code-first workflows
